About Me
======

Hi there! My name is Pam Stark, and I am a Ph.D. student and [Knight-Hennessy Scholar](https://knight-hennessy.stanford.edu/people/pam-stark) at Stanford University. My research interests lie at the intersection of experimental astroparticle physics and quantum info science. In more technical terms, I use quantum sensing technology to build detectors that search for the elusive dark matter that makes up 70% of our universe. 

On this website, you can find information about my research interests, random thoughts about science, and my perspectives on equity and inclusion within academic physics. For further inquiries, please reach out to me by email (through my sidebar) or follow me on Twitter :)

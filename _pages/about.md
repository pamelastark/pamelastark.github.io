About Me
======

Hi there! My name is Pam Stark, and I am a Physics Ph.D. student and [Knight-Hennessy Scholar](https://knight-hennessy.stanford.edu/people/pam-stark) at Stanford University. My research attempts to answer big questions about our universe by studying its smallest constituents: particles. My current research interests lie at the intersection of particle physics and quantum information science with the DMQIS group at Stanford. In the past, I worked on the [CUORE](https://cuore.lngs.infn.it) collaboration searching for neutrinoless double beta decay.

On this website, you can find information about my research interests, random thoughts about science, and my perspectives on equity and inclusion within academic physics. For further inquiries, please reach out to me by email (through my sidebar) or follow me on Twitter :)
